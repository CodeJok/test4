Real-Time Moderation
ModGuard monitors your server 24/7, instantly detecting and acting on spam, offensive language, and rule violations. Our advanced algorithms ensure that inappropriate content is flagged or removed before it disrupts your community.
Customizable Filters
Tailor ModGuard to your community’s needs with fully customizable word, link, and spam filters. Add or remove keywords, set up whitelists, and adjust sensitivity levels to strike the perfect balance between safety and freedom.
Automated Actions
Choose from a range of automated responses: delete messages, mute users, issue warnings, or apply temporary bans. ModGuard’s automation saves moderators time and ensures consistent enforcement of your rules.
Comprehensive Logging
Every moderation action is logged in detail, including user, timestamp, and reason. Access logs via a secure dashboard to review incidents, resolve disputes, and maintain transparency.
User Reports
Empower your community to report problematic messages or users directly to moderators. ModGuard collects and organizes reports, making it easy for your team to review and respond efficiently.
Role-Based Permissions
Assign different moderation powers to different roles. Fine-tune who can manage filters, view logs, or take action, ensuring your team operates smoothly and securely.
Multi-Platform Support
ModGuard works seamlessly across Discord, Slack, and more. Manage all your communities from a single interface, with consistent moderation tools everywhere.
Easy Setup & Dashboard
Get started in minutes with our intuitive setup wizard. The ModGuard dashboard provides real-time stats, configuration options, and quick access to all moderation tools.
Regular Updates & Support
Our team is dedicated to keeping ModGuard up-to-date with the latest moderation trends and threats. Enjoy regular feature updates and responsive support whenever you need help.
